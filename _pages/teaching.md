# Teaching Experience

### UCLouvain — École Polytechnique de Louvain (EPL), Belgium

From 2014 to 2021, I accumulated **200 hours of teaching experience** in undergraduate and master-level courses at the Engineering Faculty of UCLouvain (École Polytechnique de Louvain, EPL). My responsibilities included leading exercise sessions, designing assignments, and grading coursework.
